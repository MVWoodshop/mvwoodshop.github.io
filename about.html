About the MV Woodshop Club


The Woodshop club is not only a great way to explore a woodworking, but it is also a great way to meet new people! You do not need to have woodworking experience, anyone can join! It is a great place to work on personal projects, hang out with friends, and learn new wood working techniques!

Have any questions or concerns? Email us at mvwoodshopclub@gmail.com!
