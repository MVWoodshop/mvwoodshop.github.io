Club Officers for 2016-2017


  • Dara Woo - President

    The Monta Vista Woodshop Club founder and president is Dara Woo. She is currently a Senior at Monta Vista and has been running the club since the beginning of Sophomore year. Dara first got interested in Woodshop after helping her grandpa re-build their house fence one summer, and has been loving the craft ever since. In her free time, Dara enjoys judo, hanging out with her friends, and eating burritos.

  • Jamsheed Mistry - Vice President

    The vice president for this year's Woodshop Club is none other than Jamsheed Mistry. He loves hanging out with his brother and enjoys traveling to Canada. Jamsheed was interested in woodshop after previous experience in middle school and also helping out a class as a T/A. He is working on combing woodshop with biology related things and hopes to continue on his projects throughout this year too!

  • Ganesh Valliappan - Webmaster and Treasurer

    Ganesh Valliappan, the Webmaster and Treasurer for this year's woodshop club. He enjoys coding (obviously, that's why he got this job), but outside of programming, he loves reading Game of Thrones and listening to Rebelution's latest albums. He discovered his interest in woodshop during freshman year through his friend Jamsheed and has continued it since then. He hopes to have a great year in Woodshop building new and exciting projects.
